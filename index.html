About PAS Sustainability

At Pacific American School (PAS), one of our main goals is to ensure that we preserve the environment by maintaining the sustainability of our resources and environment. To help solve the issue of recycling, we decided to do research on the topic and create a project. One of the main issues that we noticed is the failure to recycle waste products that need to be recycled. After doing a survey, we realized that one of the main reasons why some students do not recycle is because there is no recycling bin or because it is not convenient for them to recycle something. To solve this problem, we created a game and a special recycling bin to give students an incentive to recycle.
